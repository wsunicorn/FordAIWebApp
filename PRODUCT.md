## Product Purpose

Website nay la kenh tu van ca nhan cua anh Huỳnh Đang Huy, giup khach tham khao thong tin xe Ford, so sanh phien ban, uoc tinh chi phi lan banh/tra gop, hoi AI cac cau hoi pho bien va de lai thong tin de anh Huy lien he.

Thanh cong cua san pham duoc do bang so lead chat luong, ty le khach bam goi/Zalo/form, thoi gian phan hoi cua sales, do tin cay cua du lieu xe/gia, va kha nang cap nhat noi dung ma khong can sua code.

Website khong phai san mua ban xe truc tuyen. MVP khong xu ly dat coc, giu xe, ky hop dong hay thanh toan online. Moi giao dich chinh thuc phai di qua anh Huy hoac dai ly theo quy trinh duoc xac nhan.

## Brand Personality

Tin cay, ro rang, tan tam.

Giong noi nen binh tinh, de hieu, thuc te va co trach nhiem. Khong tao ap luc mua xe. Khong dung ngon ngu qua kieu quang cao. Moi gia, uu dai, ton kho va lich giao xe can co nguon, ngay cap nhat va ghi chu neu chi la tham khao.

## Anti-references

Khong duoc trong nhu website chinh thuc cua Ford Viet Nam neu chua co uy quyen.

Khong duoc trong nhu san thuong mai dien tu co gio hang, thanh toan, giu xe online trong MVP.

Khong dung giao dien AI/SaaS chung chung: nen toi voi gradient tim xanh, hero trung tam lon, ba card tinh nang bang nhau, icon trang tri lap lai.

Khong sao chep nguyen van noi dung/hinh anh tu website khac neu chua co quyen su dung.

Khong de AI tu y chot gia, hua uu dai, xac nhan ton kho hoac cam ket vay.

## Design Principles

1. Trust before persuasion: thong tin ve anh Huy, don vi cong tac, nguon du lieu, ngay cap nhat va kenh lien he phai hien ro.
2. Consultation over checkout: moi luong chinh phai dan den tu van, goi/Zalo, form lead hoac lich lai thu, khong dan den mua online.
3. Mobile-first lead capture: khach xem bang dien thoai la chinh, nen CTA phai de bam, form ngan, noi dung quet nhanh.
4. Data has an owner: gia, uu dai, ton kho, phi va lai suat phai co nguon va nguoi xac nhan.
5. AI stays humble: AI giai thich, goi y va tom tat lead, nhung chuyen sang anh Huy khi can gia chot, ton kho, uu dai rieng, giu xe, ho so vay hoac khieu nai.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Muc tieu toi thieu la WCAG 2.2 AA cho cac man hinh cong khai va form lead.

Thiet ke can co tuong phan ro, font de doc tren dien thoai, nut bam lon, focus state ro, label form day du, thong bao loi gan truong nhap, va khong phu thuoc vao mau sac de truyen dat trang thai.

Motion can co muc do vua phai, uu tien feedback va chuyen trang thai. Tat hoac giam motion khi nguoi dung bat `prefers-reduced-motion`.

Noi dung uu tien tieng Viet ro rang. Neu co tieng Anh ky thuat, can giai thich bang tieng Viet.
