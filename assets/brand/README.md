This is a custom personal mark for Huỳnh Đang Huy. It is intentionally not an official Ford logo and does not mimic the Ford oval, Ford script, or official corporate lockup.

Use this asset for MVP until written approval is available for any official Ford/dealership logo usage.

## Brand Usage Decision

- Display name: Huỳnh Đang Huy.
- Role: Tư Vấn Bán Hàng.
- Dealer/unit text: Đồng Tháp Ford.
- Temporary public URL: `https://huy-ford-dong-thap.pages.dev`.
- Official Ford/dealership logo, showroom photos, catalogues and vehicle media: treat as source assets that require permission before public reuse.

## Do Not

- Do not create a logo that looks similar to Ford's oval/script logo.
- Do not present the MVP website as an official Ford Vietnam website unless authorization is confirmed.
- Do not copy showroom, customer, catalogue or vehicle images into the public site without a permission record.
